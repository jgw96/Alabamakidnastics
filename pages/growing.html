We will be adding a new class for our late 2’s/early 3’s. The cost will be $180 per month. This curriculum is very interactive and teaches the beginning preschool skills, i.e. coloring, circle time, learning through song, etc. Each child will receive a kit each month containing all the necessary supplies for simple class projects, stories, and a parent newsletter to keep you in the loop. The registration fee for this class will be $110 after Juyl1st fee goes up to $125. This will cover their kits for August thru May and the everyday supplies for their classroom.
