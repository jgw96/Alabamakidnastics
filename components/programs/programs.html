Gymnastics

Alabama Kidnastics is a complete gymnastics program teaching skills on bars, beam, floor, vault, and rings. We take children from the age of 2 and up for boys and girls. We teach a progression of skills on each piece of equipment. In the classes students will develop flexibility, coordination, strength and agility.

Gymnastics Team

As we teach a progression of skills in our classes, it prepares the student for our competitive team. Team is a year round commitment. Our competitive season begins in September and goes thru March. April thru August we are working on compulsory routines on bars, beam, floor and vault. Our team girls practice 6 hours per week. Our competitions are local and extend to Nashville, Birmingham, Atlanta, and Tuscaloosa. If you have a child that would be interested please ask us about a tryout. Most children can compete with beginner skills because the routines are choreographed in a progression.

Parkour/Ninja Warrior

We are bringing the outdoors inside and providing children with the opportunity to run, jump, swing, climb and play. This class combines a little of gymnastics, with fitness and parkour. In this class, we will safely introduce to the basic of parkour/gymnastics/ fitness through landing, rolling, vaulting, climbing and moving over, under, across and through various obstacles.
