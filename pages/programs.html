Kids Night Out

6pm-10pm

We will offer Kid's Night Out one Saturday of every month. Bring your children to us and have a night out or just a quiet evening at home. We provide fun organized activities with dinner included. Children will pariticipate in gymnastics, parachute, relays and games. We will end the evening with a movie. This event is for 1 year olds to 12 year olds.
